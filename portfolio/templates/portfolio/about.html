About me:

Executive Summary:

Aspiring to become a software engineer, I'm in the junior year of a B.S. in Computer Science at Western Governor's University. Received a certificate in Geospatial Information Systems (GIS) after learning drone flight, spatial analysis, and Python scripting which I used to build an internal tool for the Oregon Metro Data Research Center. Fluent in Mandarin Chinese and culturally aware after living in China for four years. Formerly a restaurant manager, once a language teacher, I bring a unique background to the table that combines international context, situational dexterity, empathetic communication, and technical skill.
